Our Story

Welcome to Parkgrove Counselling, a safe and confidential space where you can talk about issues that are affecting you. We will help you explore your ways of thinking and being, and support you to find ways to live your life in a way that feels right for you. We will help you shine a light on repeating patterns of behaviour or thinking that may be holding you back.

About Katie

There are many reasons why you may be considering counselling. Are you feeling unhappy, hurt, confused or lacking in self-confidence? Perhaps you feel that you are going around in circles trying to find answers to a personal dilemma? Are you struggling to understand your feelings and emotions, or having difficulties relating to those around you?

Is there something troubling you from your past or in your present?
